Description

Today were conquering our fears and making homemade croissants! If you’re about to run away screaming, I understand. I’m not sugarcoating it: croissants aren’t easy. Croissants require time, patience, and a lot of rolling. However, just because this recipe is advanced doesn’t mean that YOU have to be an advanced baker to try it. You can absolutely handle this recipe.

  • 11/4 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 3 tablespoons warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 2/3cup warm milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2/3cup unsalted butter, chilled
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon water

directions

step 1

Combine yeast, warm water, and 1 teaspoon sugar. Allow to stand until creamy and frothy.
